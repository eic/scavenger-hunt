---
title: 'Learner Profiles'
---

This lesson targets ePIC collaboration members — students, postdocs, and researchers — who already
have a working `eic-shell` and want to practise the full workflow of finding, simulating, and
analysing ePIC data. It assumes basic familiarity with the shell and ROOT, but walks through each
step of browsing campaign files, running simulations, inspecting detector geometry, and analysing
reconstruction output as a series of self-contained tasks.
