If you get stuck on any task, or want to discuss the scavenger hunt with other participants, ask in
the [Scavenger Hunt channel on Mattermost](https://chat.epic-eic.org/main/channels/scavenger-hunt).

For general questions about the EIC software, see the
[ePIC getting-started guide](https://eic.github.io/documentation/getstarted), the list of
[ePIC tutorials](https://eic.github.io/documentation/tutorials.html), and the
[software-tutorials channel on Mattermost](https://chat.epic-eic.org/main/channels/software-tutorials).
